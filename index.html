Laika: Aged Through Blood

Laika: Aged Through Blood is an action-adventure Metroidvania set in a post-apocalyptic western world. Play as Laika, a coyote mother and fierce warrior, and carve your motorbike through a land ravaged by betrayal and loss as you uncover dark secrets in the pursuit of vengeance against oppression.

 

I was responsible for porting the Steam version of the game to Nintendo Switch, working closely with the original development team to technically adapt the project and tweak several mechanics to fit the specific requirements of the Nintendo Switch platform. I was also responsible for the updated PlayStation 5 version and for bringing the game to its physical PS5 release published by Super Rare Games.

Tiny Garden

Tiny Garden lets you grow a pocket-sized garden inside a magical toy passed down through generations. Nurture plants, trade them for furniture, and customize your space in this relaxing mix of farming, puzzles, and strategy. No scores or time limits — let your creativity bloom!

 

I served as the Technical Artist for the game, where I designed and implemented all VFX and animations, blending technical execution with creative vision to enrich the game's atmosphere.

Die or Die

Roll the dice and choose how you will die. Die or Die is a top-down shooter roguelite where you run, shoot and dodge enemies to get dice with which to disable threats or unlock abilities. Roll. Die. Repeat.

 

I led the creation of the initial prototype and collaborated during production to refine the game feel and ensure the project met its technical goals.

Cannibal Crossing

Cannibal Crossing is an action-survival game where you explore the randomly generated town of Earlsboro while scavenging, fighting hordes of enemies, building a base, and searching for a way to escape! Meet survivors with new abilities and lots of cannibals. Includes a co-op mode for 1-4 players!

 

I was involved in the original development of the game, playing a hands-on role in core areas like design, programming, feel, and VFX, helping shape the game's identity from the ground up.
